Welcome to Securanote

Securanote is your encrypted digital vault for notes and files. It uses advanced cryptographic techniques such as AES and ChaCha20 to secure your content.

Your data is safely stored in AWS S3. Share notes through secure, view-once links or dynamic QR codes, giving you full control over who sees what and when.
